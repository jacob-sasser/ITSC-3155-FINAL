About Us

At HelpOut, our core mission is to be a beacon of support for university students navigating the often complex landscape of academia. We understand the challenges and uncertainties that students face when making critical decisions that shape their educational journey and future prospects. Our aim is to alleviate these burdens by offering comprehensive guidance and resources. We are dedicated to empowering students in their decision-making processes, whether it's choosing the right course, selecting suitable extracurricular activities, exploring various career paths, or finding the perfect fit for higher education. We believe that informed decisions lead to fulfilling academic experiences and successful futures. Moreover, our commitment extends beyond mere guidance. We are fervently devoted to unlocking scholarship opportunities for deserving students. We recognize the transformative power of education and strive to ensure that financial constraints do not hinder academic excellence. Through our extensive network and tailored assistance, we endeavor to connect students with a multitude of scholarship avenues, easing the financial burden and opening doors to limitless possibilities. Ultimately, HelpOut stands as a dedicated ally, empowering students to not just survive but thrive in their academic pursuits. We aspire to be the catalyst for positive change, fostering a community where every student has the tools, support, and opportunities needed to excel in their educational endeavors and beyond.

Our Team

Meet the dynamic founders behind HelpOut: Hy, Luis, Sushant, and Jacob, a passionate group of university students committed to making a meaningful impact in the academic realm. With diverse backgrounds and a shared drive for change, these founders united with a common goal: to make the ultimate support system for fellow students. Hy, with a hunger for innovative solutions, Luis, driven by a passion for education equality, Sushant, a strategic thinker with a heart for empowering others, and Jacob, a visionary bridging ideas with action, each brings unique perspectives and skills to the table. Together, they created HelpOut, a platform dedicated to guiding and uplifting students through every challenge, decision, and opportunity along their educational journey.
